ABOUT US

Kent catfish is a group owned catfish farm that grows table sized catfish by using feeds of high nutritious quality and fresh water.We process our catfish of different sizes(kg) in a hygenic environment by smoking and drying methodically.We package the the catfish and smoked fish and sell according to the size if the catfish(kg).We sell in wholesale and retail prices all around Abuja(Fct) as well as Nassarawa,Kogi,Niger,Kaduna and environs.

Value
We provide a good source of fresh water protein.

Mission
Providing nutritious table sized fish to one person at a time.Satisfying the needs of our customers

Vision
To provide a go to nutritious fish hub in Abuja for Nigerians and Africa at large.
